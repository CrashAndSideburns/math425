# Subspace Topology
## Definition
Let $\left(X, \tau_{X}\right)$ be a [topological space](topological_space), and let $Y \subseteq X$. Define $\tau_{Y}=\left\{U \cap Y: U \in \tau_{X}\right\}$. $\tau_{Y}$ is then a [topology](topology) on $Y$, called the *subspace topology*.
